Experience

I have experience in C/Matlab programming, Verilog coding and PCB layout design. And I am familiar with Xilinx FPGA. I participated in several projects and published some articles.

  • building an experimental facility for a quantum information research group.

    We need to capture electric signal from several quantum bits, process the data and adjust the quantum bits’status in realtime. Different from the traditional Fourier transformation or Laplace transformation, the physicists propose various transformation to process the data. So we try to build the whole digital system in FPGAs. The workload increase greatly with the number of quantum bits. So building efficient signal processing system is the key problem of the whole experimental facility.
